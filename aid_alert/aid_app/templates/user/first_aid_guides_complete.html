Emergency First Aid Instructions

Life-saving medical guidance for critical situations. Always call emergency services first.

Emergency (India): 112
Poison Control (India): 1800-11-0442

Quick Access Guides

Select an emergency situation below for step-by-step instructions

CPR

Life-Threatening
1
Check Responsiveness

Tap shoulder and shout. Check breathing.

2
Call 112

Call immediately if unresponsive.

3
Compressions

30 chest compressions at 100-120/min.

4
Rescue Breaths

2 breaths after 30 compressions.

Note: Continue until help arrives.
sick

Choking

Life-Threatening
1
Assess

Can they speak or cough?

2
Back Blows

5 blows between shoulder blades.

3
Abdominal Thrusts

5 inward and upward thrusts above navel.

Infants: Use chest thrusts instead.
bloodtype

Severe Bleeding

Urgent
1
Direct Pressure

Apply firm pressure with clean cloth.

2
Elevate

Raise injured area above heart level.

Warning: Seek immediate medical help.
local_fire_department

Burns

Moderate
1
Cool Water

Run cool water for 10-20 mins.

2
Cover

Use sterile, non-stick dressing.

Don't: Use ice or greasy ointments.
psychology

Stroke (FAST)

Life-Threatening
1
Face

Drooping on one side?

2
Arms

One arm drifting down?

3
Speech

Slurred or strange speech?

4
Time

Call 911 immediately.

medical_services

Fractures

Moderate
1
Immobilize

Keep area still. Don't try to realign.

2
Ice

Apply cold pack to reduce swelling.

monitor_heart

Heart Attack

Life-Threatening
1
Call 911

Call immediately. Do not drive yourself.

2
Chew Aspirin

Chew 325mg aspirin if not allergic.

3
Rest

Sit or lie down while waiting.

Symptoms: Chest pain, shortness of breath.
bolt

Seizure

Life-Threatening
1
Protect Head

Cushion head, move dangerous objects.

2
Do Not Restrain

Let the seizure run its course.

3
Recovery Position

Roll onto side after seizure stops.

Never: Put anything in their mouth.
warning

Poisoning

Urgent
1
Call Help

Call Poison Control (1-800-222-1222).

2
Identify Poison

Check labels or surroundings.

3
Follow Instructions

Do NOT induce vomiting unless told.

coronavirus

Allergic Reaction

Life-Threatening
1
Use Epipen

Inject into outer thigh if available.

2
Call 911

Seek emergency care immediately.

3
Monitor

Watch for breathing difficulties.

Signs: Swelling, hives, trouble breathing.
wb_sunny

Heat Exhaustion

Moderate
1
Cool Down

Move to shade or air conditioning.

2
Hydrate

Drink cool water or sports drinks.

3
Rest

Lie down and loosen clothing.

ac_unit

Hypothermia

Urgent
1
Move to Warmth

Get indoors/shelter. Remove wet clothes.

2
Warm Slowly

Use blankets and warm (not hot) drinks.

3
Share Heat

Body-to-body contact if necessary.

Avoid: Hot water or rubbing skin.
